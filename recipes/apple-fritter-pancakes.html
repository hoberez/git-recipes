Apple Fritter Pancakes Recipe

Description

Apple fritter pancakes make a wonderful breakfast topped with some warmed maple syrup and served with some breakfast meats. You can use any type of apple for this recipeâ€” I used Honeycrisp (Fuji works great, too), but the sweeter the better. If you are using a tart apple, add another tablespoon of sugar.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground ginger
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup shredded apple
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour, or as needed
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/8 teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Combine egg, sugar cinammon, ginger, nutmeg, kosher salt, and vanilla extract in a bowl. Whisk until well combined and lightly foamy. Add shredded apple and lemon juice and fold in with a spatula until well combined. Add flour, baking powder, and baking soda. Mix until flour disappears and batter is thick yet spoonable.
  3. Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Add tablespoonfuls of apple batter to the hot butter and cook until browned, about 3 minutes. Turn and cook until apple pancakes spring back to the touch and are browned on the other side, an additional 3 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
